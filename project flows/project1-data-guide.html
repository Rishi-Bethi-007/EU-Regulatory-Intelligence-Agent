multilingual-e5-large — Your Core Embedding Model
Sentence Transformer · 560MB · 100+ Languages
Free HuggingFace Auto-downloads on first run
HuggingFace Model Page
huggingface.co/intfloat/multilingual-e5-large
→ pip install sentence-transformers
→ Auto-cached to: ~/.cache/huggingface/
→ First load: ~30 seconds. After: instant.
Integration Code
from langchain_community.embeddings import HuggingFaceEmbeddings

embeddings = HuggingFaceEmbeddings(model_name="intfloat/multilingual-e5-large")

Drop-in replacement for OpenAIEmbeddings. Runs locally — no API cost, no data leaves your machine. Output: 1024-dim vectors. Update your Supabase vector(1024) column type accordingly.
🔍
multilingual-e5-large-instruct — Query-Optimised Variant
Instruction-Tuned Variant · Better for Queries
Free HuggingFace
HuggingFace Model Page
huggingface.co/intfloat/multilingual-e5-large-instruct
→ Use this for QUERY embeddings
→ Use base model for DOCUMENT embeddings
When to Use
Use the instruct variant when embedding the user's query. Use the base variant for document chunks. This asymmetric setup is the correct way to use E5 models and gives better retrieval quality. Prefix queries with: "query: what are the EU AI Act obligations...". Mention this in your README — it shows model-level thinking.

Exact Questions to Write
Write exactly these 20 Q&A pairs in evals/qa_pairs.json:

EU AI Act (English) — 8 questions:
"What are the prohibited AI practices under Article 5?"
"Which sectors are listed in Annex III as high-risk?"
"What does Article 13 require for transparency?"
"What fines can be imposed under the AI Act?"
"What is a conformity assessment under Article 43?"
"How does the AI Act define a general purpose AI model?"
"What obligations apply to AI system providers?"
"When did the EU AI Act enter into force?"

GDPR (English) — 6 questions:
"What is the right to erasure under Article 17?"
"What constitutes lawful processing under Article 6?"
"What are the data minimization principles?"
"What must a privacy notice include?"
"What is a Data Protection Impact Assessment?"
"What are the breach notification requirements?"

Swedish language — 3 questions:
"Vad säger AI-förordningen om riskklassificering?"
"Vilka krav ställer GDPR på dataminimering?"
"Vad är DIGG:s roll i AI-reglering?"

German language — 3 questions:
"Was sind die Anforderungen der KI-Verordnung für Hochrisiko-KI?"
"Welche Strafen sieht der AI Act vor?"
"Was versteht man unter Transparenzpflichten?"
How to Use in RAGAS
For each Q&A pair, write the ground truth answer yourself by reading the source document. Store as {"question": "...", "answer": "...", "contexts": ["..."], "ground_truth": "..."}. RAGAS scores your system's answers against these. Target: faithfulness > 0.75, answer relevancy > 0.70. These numbers go directly in your README and CV.

RAGAS Synthetic Testset Generator — Auto-Generate More Q&A Pairs
Automated Eval Dataset Generation
Free (uses your OpenAI/Claude API) RAGAS Library
How to Generate
After ingesting your EU AI Act PDF, run:

from ragas.testset import TestsetGenerator
from ragas.testset.evolutions import simple, reasoning, multi_context

generator = TestsetGenerator.with_openai()
testset = generator.generate_with_langchain_docs(docs, test_size=20, distributions={simple: 0.5, reasoning: 0.3, multi_context: 0.2})

This auto-generates 20 more Q&A pairs from your EU AI Act document.
What You Get
Combines with your 20 manual pairs for a 40+ pair evaluation dataset. The synthetic generator creates multi-hop questions that test whether your retriever can find information that spans multiple chunks — much harder than single-chunk retrieval. Document the generation process in your README. Save the testset as evals/testset_generated.json.

What the Agent Will Search Live

• "EU AI Act manufacturing compliance 2024 2025"
• "GDPR AI systems obligations Sweden"
• "EU AI Act Annex III high-risk systems examples"
• "EU AI Act fines enforcement cases"

These live results are scraped, chunked, and fed into your RAG pipeline alongside your pre-ingested documents — giving you both stable reference knowledge and current news.
Your Custom Scraper MCP Handles This
Your Web Scraper MCP server (Phase 4) will fetch and clean these search results. The pipeline is: Brave Search → top 5 URLs → your scraper MCP → cleaned markdown → chunk → retrieve. This means your agent is always working with current information, not just your pre-ingested PDFs. Critical for regulatory topics that change.
